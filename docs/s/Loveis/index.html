About the Song

SOULS AT PLAY captures the ethereal connection between two souls who find joy and freedom in each other's presence. This romantic and transcendent piece portrays love as a cosmic dance, where hearts open wide and spirits soar beyond earthly limitations.

The song evokes feelings of bliss and serenity through imagery of laughter on the breeze, hands touching stars, and the rhythm of eternal tides. It's an exploration of intimacy and the unspoken understanding that exists between two people deeply in love.
