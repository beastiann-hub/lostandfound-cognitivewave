The Story Behind the Song

Sometimes the most beautiful music emerges from our deepest longing. "CARE" was born from a guitar riff that lived in my heart for years, waiting for the perfect moment to bloom into something meaningful.

That moment came during a quiet, sleepless night when my love was away for two weeks. In the silence, with only my thoughts and that persistent melody, I found myself crafting something that felt both deeply personal and universally human.

What started as a message to someone special became something larger—a song for everyone who has ever felt the ache of distance, the beauty of missing someone, and the hope that connects us even when we're apart.

A Song for All Who Long

"CARE" is more than just a song—it's a bridge between hearts separated by distance, a gentle reminder that love transcends physical space, and a warm embrace for anyone who has ever stared at the ceiling during those quiet, contemplative hours when the world sleeps but your heart stays awake, reaching across the darkness for connection.
