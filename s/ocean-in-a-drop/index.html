About the Song

You’re Bleeding Hard lives in the fragile space between vulnerability and detachment. Hypnotic synths, glassy guitar lines and heartbeat drums frame a voice that wavers between confession and defense.

Shaded by chillwave textures and dream-pop atmosphere, the track turns melancholy into something quietly luminous — the sound of holding yourself together when everything inside wants to fall apart.
