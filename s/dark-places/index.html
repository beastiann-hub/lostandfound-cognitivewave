About the Track

“Dark Places” is a reflection on the battles we fight when no one is watching — the quiet storms, the restless nights, and the fragile sparks of hope that keep us moving. It’s about confronting your shadows and realizing that even in the darkest corners, there’s beauty waiting to be found. The song invites you to slow down, breathe, and embrace the moments that make us human — imperfect, emotional, and endlessly resilient.
